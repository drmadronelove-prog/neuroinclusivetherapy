HOW TO PLAY
🏃 MOVE

Hold → RIGHT to run. ↑ JUMP to leap over obstacles. ↓ DUCK to dodge mid-height threats. You stop when you let go.

✨ THE SPARK

Your inner spark is your score and your story. Hits and draining yeses dim it. If it goes out before you reach the end, the level ends.

🧱 NO

When someone appears, press N to drop the wall. Watch it explode. Keep moving. Every no is a win.

✋ YES

Press Y to say yes. The consequence is always a surprise. Three yeses in a row triggers overwhelm.

You don't have to be rude. You just have to keep going.
